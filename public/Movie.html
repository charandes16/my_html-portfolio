The Best Movies According to Charan

My top 3 Movies of all-time

Kaithi (Tamil)

I enjoyed the intense suspense and gripping storytelling in the 2019 movie "Kaithi."

2018 (Malayalam)

I appreciate Tovino Thomas's compelling performance and the film's exploration of complex moral dilemmas in the movie "2018".

Leo (Tamil)

As a fan of "Thalapathy", I admire the charisma and screen presence Vijay brings to the movie "Leo," elevating the narrative with his magnetic performance.
